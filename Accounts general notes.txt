Savings
	non-negative balance
	monthly interest (global rate)
	service fees (global)
	no restrictions

CDs
	globally set minimum balance
	several term lengths, after which interest stops
	interest set based on term, when created
	withdrawal penalties
	no service fees

Checking
	globally set overdraft limit
	overdraft service charge
	service charge below minimum balance

Loans
	negative balance
	monthly interest, set when created
	minimum payment or penalty fee
	base interest rate

Line of credit
	negative balance
	limit set per-account
	interest accrues monthly
	deposits must be made monthly, or penalty fee
